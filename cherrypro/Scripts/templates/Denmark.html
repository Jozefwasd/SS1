Top 5 places to visit in Denmark

Number 1: Tivoli Gardens

Tivoli Gardens in Copenhagen has been one of the most popular attractions in Denmark
for a very long time. Second oldest amusement park in the world but still a fan favorite.

Number 2: Bakken - oldest fairground in the world!

Just north of Copenhagen you will find Dyrehavsbakken (Bakken) which is the world's oldest
operating amusement park. With free entry this amusement park is also a very popular attraction in Denmark.

Number 3: Legoland!

One of the most popular attraction in Denmark.

Number 4: Copenhagen Zoo!

Elephants, hippos, lions, monkeys and polar bears. A visit to Copenhagen's Zoo in the Frederiksberg area is always a wild and exciting experience.
Expect to see a lot of native Danes in the Zoo as it remains a very popular attraction in Denmark among Copenhagen families.

Number 5: Djurs Summerland!

Bring your entire family to Djurs Sommerland! Days of fun for all ages! Ready for speed, thrills and fun for all the family?
One of the best attractions in Denmark for cooling off in the summer heat.
